Defend The Canon

You are alone in the darkness, surrounded by strange orbs that close in from every side. Your bullets are your only source of light. But beware — the very enemies you destroy may harm you as much as they heal you. Balance caution with courage, and survive as long as you can.

Rules

  1. Shooting each orb gives you +100 points.
  2. When enemies reach you, they take away 100 points.
  3. If your score drops to -100, the game is over.
  4. Try to beat your own high score every time.

Controls

  1. Right Arrow / Scroll Up → Rotate cannon clockwise.
  2. Left Arrow / Scroll Down → Rotate cannon counter-clockwise.
  3. Space / Left-Click → Shoot.
